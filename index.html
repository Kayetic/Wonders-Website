What is it?

The Great Pyramid of Giza is an ancient wonder located in Egypt, built over 4,500 years ago. It took over 20 years to construct using millions of stone blocks and stands over 140 meters tall. The Pyramid is a tomb for the Pharaoh Khufu, and remains an iconic symbol of ancient Egyptian engineering.
